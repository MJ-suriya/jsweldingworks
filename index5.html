About Us

Welcome to JS Welding Works - Your Premier Destination for Quality Welding and Fabrication Services in Southern Tamil Nadu!

Established in 2020 by Mr. A. Muthu Ramalingam, JS Welding Works specializes in gate works, shed installations, hand railing, and other fabrication services. Serving clients across southern Tamil Nadu, including Tuticorin and Tirunelveli, we are committed to delivering top-notch products and services at competitive prices.

Under Mr. Ramalingam's leadership, we prioritize quality and affordability, ensuring customer satisfaction with every project. From constructing sheds to hand railing installations, we guarantee durability and functionality in all our work.

As we expand our operations, our mission remains unchanged: to provide premium quality services that meet the diverse needs of our customers. Partner with JS Welding Works for all your welding and fabrication requirements, and experience excellence at every step.

Our Services

Materials

We have extensive experience working with a wide range of materials, including steel, aluminum, stainless steel,GA metals and exotic alloys. Our specialized expertise allows us to handle diverse welding projects with precision and efficiency.

Welding Services

We specialize in Arc welding. Arc welding is a versatile and widely used welding technique that involves the use of an electric arc to melt and join metals. It is suitable for welding a wide range of materials, including steel,GA metal and stainless steel. Our expertise in Arc welding allows us to deliver high-quality welds for diverse applications, ensuring durability and structural integrity.

Grill Gates Making and Fabrication

We specialize in grill gates making and fabrication, crafting durable and stylish gates tailored to your specifications. Using high-quality metals like steel and stainless steel, we ensure robust construction and long-lasting performance. Our team collaborates closely with customers to understand their requirements and preferences, delivering custom-designed grill gates that enhance security and aesthetics while ensuring satisfaction.

MS and Stainless Steel Handrailing

We specialize in crafting handrails from MS and stainless steel, providing sturdy and elegant solutions for safety and aesthetics. Our handrails are custom-designed to fit your space and style preferences, ensuring both functionality and visual appeal. With a focus on quality and precision, we deliver handrailings that meet the highest standards of durability and customer satisfaction.

Cooling Sheet Shed Works

We specialize in designing and fabricating cooling sheet sheds for various industries. Whether small-scale or large industrial, our sheds offer effective temperature control and protection for equipment and materials. We tailor designs for optimal airflow, energy efficiency, and durability, ensuring the highest standards of performance and satisfaction.

Asbestos Sheet Works

We specialize in asbestos sheet works, offering expert fabrication and installation services for various applications. Our team ensures precise cutting, shaping, and installation of asbestos sheets, adhering to safety regulations and industry standards. From roofing to insulation, we provide durable and reliable solutions tailored to your needs.
